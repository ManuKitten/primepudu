What is PrimePudu?

PrimePudu is a free app with various math contents presented in a game learning style, with videos and text for many different people to learn. It is aimed for people with low knowledge of math who want or need to understand the diverse math themes.


PrimePudu's lessons are aimed at being totally understandable, with many examples to make sure the content is learned.
